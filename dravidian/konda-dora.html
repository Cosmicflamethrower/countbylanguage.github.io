Konda-Dora

Dravidian > South Central > Gondi-Kui > Konda-Kui > Konda-Dora

Konda-Dora, also simply known as Konda, is a Dravidian language spoken in the Indian state of Andhra Pradesh. Due to its close proximity to Telugu, it has adopted the Telugu script and all numbers except 1-4 are Telugu. However, a small population in the state of Odisha also use the Odia script.
