Proto-Dravidian

Dravidian

Proto-Dravidian is the ancestor of all Dravidian languages. No records of it survive, so Proto-Dravidian is reconstructed from the languages it has produced.

Here are the numbers from zero to ten translated in Proto-Dravidian:

Number Romanisation IPA*
0 //
1 onṯu /ontu/
2 iraṇṭu /iraɳʈu/
3 mūnt̠u /muːntu/
4 nāl /naːl/
5 caymtu /cajmt̪u/
6 cāṯu /caːtu/
7 eẓu /eːɻu/
8 eṇṭṭu /eɳʈːu/
9 toḷ/toṇ /t̪oɭ/t̪oɳ/
10 paHtu /paht̪u/

*Proto-Dravidian phonemes are still a matter of debate. The provided IPA transcriptions may not be accurate.
